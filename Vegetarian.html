GOA'S VEGETARIAN DISHES

Though Goa is best known for its seafood, its vegetarian cuisine is deeply rooted in local tradition. Influenced by seasonal produce and temple cooking, Goan vegetarian dishes use coconut, jaggery, kokum, and spices to create unique flavors. Favorites include Khatkhate (a festive mixed vegetable stew), Tambdi Bhaji (red amaranth stir-fry), and Alsande Tonak (black-eyed pea curry). Dishes are typically served with steamed rice, pickle, and papad. During festivals, pure vegetarian thalis are prepared with love and cultural pride.


Popular Goan Vegetarian Dishes include:
  • Khatkhate – a festive mixed vegetable stew
  • Tambdi Bhaji – red amaranth stir-fry
  • Alsande Tonak – black-eyed pea curry
  • Bharli Vangi – Stuffed baby brinjals with masala
